Vacation Planner Android App

The Vacation Planner app is a mobile application designed to help users organize vacations and excursions. Users can add new vacations, view existing ones, and manage detailed information such as location, hotel, start and end dates, and associated excursions. Excursions can also be created and attached to vacations and support alerts and validation features.
